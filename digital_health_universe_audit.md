# Digital Health Universe Discovery Audit

## Mission

Build a master employer universe for digital health, healthcare analytics, health AI, population health, clinical decision support, EHR/interoperability, quality measurement, healthcare data, healthcare SaaS, precision medicine, remote patient monitoring, and value-based care companies.

## Method

- **Search engine:** Google (VM browser only)
- **Query:** `("digital health" OR "healthcare analytics" OR "health AI" OR "clinical AI" OR "population health" OR "value based care" OR "health technology" OR "healthcare software" OR "quality measurement" OR "clinical analytics" OR "health data platform" OR "precision medicine" OR "real world data" OR "remote patient monitoring" OR "care management" OR "interoperability" OR "EHR software" OR "health SaaS")`
- **Results reviewed:** Organic results only (AI Overview, sponsored, Google Jobs, and People Also Ask ignored)
- **Exclusions applied:** LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, ZipRecruiter, job postings, blogs, directories, news articles, conference pages, vendor lists, government (.gov), academic journals, professional associations
- **Click policy:** Results opened only when legitimacy could not be determined from the visible snippet
