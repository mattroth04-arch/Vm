
## Key Findings

1. **Professional associations dominate** — 12 of 43 organizations (28%) are professional associations or societies.
2. **Government agencies well represented** — 11 government organizations at federal and state levels (26%).
3. **Active consulting market** — 13 environmental/occupational health consulting firms identified (30%).
4. **Geographic breadth** — Federal (NIOSH, NCI, CHEMM), multi-state (CSTE, ASTHO, AOEC), state-level (LA, TX, NC, SD, NV, AZ, WA), and international (UNIDO, ISEE, ISIE) entities.
5. **Multidisciplinary overlap** — Many organizations span multiple mission categories (e.g., ACOEM covers occupational and environmental medicine; RHP spans exposure science, toxicology, and epidemiology).
6. **Search exhaustion** — After page 16, results increasingly returned duplicates, government labor commissions without health focus, industrial supply companies, and academic degree programs, confirming comprehensive coverage of primary organizations for this query.
