5. **URL verification:** Recorded visible URLs and extracted clean domain names for each organization
6. **Specialty extraction:** Captured brief descriptions from Google snippets or company pages to characterize each organization's focus
7. **Click-through verification:** For ambiguous cases, clicked through to company pages to verify legitimacy and services offered
