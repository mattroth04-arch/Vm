## Key Observations

1. **Low organic yield:** The Boolean OR query matched heavily on generic "digital" terms, producing government sites, education programs, and non-health technology companies rather than nutrition, microbiome, or consumer health employers.
2. **Single qualifying homepage:** Only Digital Wellness (`digitalwellness.com`) appeared as a direct company homepage in organic results across all four pages reviewed.
3. **Directory pollution:** Multiple organic results were "top companies" listicles (Wellable, Y Combinator, CoreHealth, SoHookd, Enterprise League) rather than employer homepages; these were excluded per workflow rules.
4. **AI Overview not used:** Google's AI Overview on page 1 referenced additional health companies (e.g., Viome, Noom, ZOE), but those did not appear as organic blue-link results and were excluded per instructions.
